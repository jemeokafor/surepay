Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SurePay

No, we're not an escrow service. SurePay is a buyer-protected checkout platform. We facilitate payments between buyers and vendors while holding funds securely until delivery is confirmed. Think of us as a trust layer for social commerce, not a traditional escrow.

SurePay charges vendors just 1.5% per transaction. There are no setup fees, no monthly subscriptions, and no hidden charges. Buyers pay nothing extra – the listed price is what they pay. For a ₦100,000 transaction, the vendor pays just ₦1,500.

Vendors are paid within 24 hours after the buyer confirms delivery. If the buyer doesn't respond within 24 hours of delivery being marked, the funds are automatically released. No more waiting weeks or chasing payments.

Both buyer and vendor can open a dispute with evidence. Our team reviews the case within 48 hours and makes a fair decision. Valid reasons include non-delivery, wrong item, damaged goods, or counterfeit products. We're committed to fair resolutions for both parties.
