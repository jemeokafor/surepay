Create a compelling landing page for SurePay, a buyer-protected checkout platform for Nigerian social commerce.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
From DESIGN.md Section 6:
- Color: Primary Blue #0066FF for CTAs, Success Green #10B981 for trust
- Typography: Satoshi font, Large clear headings (48px hero), 16px+ body
- Layout: Single column mobile-first, max-width 1200px, sticky header
- Components: Rounded corners (8px-12px), subtle shadows, clear hierarchy
- Trust signals: Bank logos, security icons, Paystack badge

**Page Structure:**
1. **Sticky Header**
   - Logo: "SurePay" with shield icon
   - Nav: For Vendors, For Buyers, How It Works
   - CTA: "Get Started" (primary blue button)

2. **Hero Section**
   - Headline: "Buy with Confidence. Sell with Trust."
   - Subheadline: "Protected payments for Instagram, WhatsApp & social commerce. Your money is safe until you confirm delivery."
   - Two CTAs: "Start Selling" (primary) + "Learn More" (secondary)
   - Hero image: Show phone with SurePay checkout + happy vendor/buyer

3. **Trust Bar**
   - Logos: "Secured by Paystack" + "CBN Compliant" + "Bank-Level Security"
   - Stats: "₦500M+ Protected" + "10,000+ Vendors" + "99.9% Success Rate"

4. **How It Works (3 steps)**
   - Step 1: "Create Payment Link" - Vendor generates custom link
   - Step 2: "Buyer Pays Safely" - Funds held securely
   - Step 3: "Release on Delivery" - Vendor gets paid after confirmation
   - Visual: Simple icons + arrows showing flow

5. **For Vendors Section**
   - Headline: "Stop Losing Sales to Distrust"
   - Benefits:
     * "Recover ghosted DMs" - 30% more conversions
     * "Build public trust" - Live badge shows your history
     * "Fast payouts" - Get paid within 24h of delivery
   - CTA: "Join 500+ Vendors" (green success color)

6. **For Buyers Section**
   - Headline: "Shop with Zero Risk"
   - Benefits:
     * "Money protected" until you confirm delivery
     * "Easy disputes" - Report issues in one click
     * "Verified vendors" - See their settlement history
   - CTA: "Find Protected Sellers"

7. **Social Proof**
   - Testimonials: 2-3 quotes from vendors
   - Trust badges: "Featured in TechCabal" + "Backed by PennPromise"

8. **FAQ Section** (accordion style)
   - "Is SurePay an escrow service?" → No, we're buyer-protected checkout
   - "How much does it cost?" → 1.5% per transaction
   - "When do I get paid?" → 24h after buyer confirms delivery
   - "What if there's a dispute?" → We mediate within 48h

9. **Footer**
   - Logo + tagline: "The safest way to buy and sell online"
   - Links: About, Terms, Privacy, Contact
   - Social: Instagram, Twitter/X, WhatsApp
   - Copyright: © 2026 SurePay

**Mobile-First Requirements:**
- Touch targets 48px+
- Sticky bottom CTA on mobile
- Collapsible hamburger menu
- Fast loading (optimize images)

**Trust Elements to Include:**
- Lock icons next to "protected" text
- Paystack badge prominently
- Nigerian Naira (₦) symbols
- "CBN Compliant" trust badge
