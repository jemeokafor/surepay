# SurePay Site Vision & Roadmap

## 1. Site Vision

**SurePay** is a buyer-protected checkout platform for Nigerian social commerce. The design must communicate:

- **Trust & Security:** Buyers must feel safe paying
- **Speed & Simplicity:** Vendors must onboard in under 5 minutes
- **Professionalism:** Financial-grade without being intimidating
- **Mobile-First:** 90%+ of users on mobile devices

## 2. Target Audience

- **Vendors:** Social sellers (20k-50k followers) in Lagos/Abuja
- **Buyers:** Skeptical consumers making NGN 50k-250k purchases
- **Admin:** Operations team handling disputes

## 3. Brand Attributes

- Trustworthy but not corporate
- Fast but not rushed
- Modern but not cold
- Nigerian but globally competent
