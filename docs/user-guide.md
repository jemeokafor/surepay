# SurePay User Guide

## Overview

SurePay is a buyer-protected payment platform designed for Nigerian social commerce. This guide explains how to use SurePay as both a vendor and a buyer.

## For Vendors

### Getting Started

1. **Sign Up**
   - Visit the SurePay website
   - Click "Sign Up" and enter your email
   - Check your email for a magic link to complete registration
   - Alternatively, sign up with Google

2. **Complete Your Profile**
   - Add your display name and username
   - Upload a profile picture (optional)
   - Write a short brand bio

3. **Set Up Payout Details**
   - Enter your bank account information
   - Verify your bank account details
   - This step is required before you can receive payments

### Managing Your Store

#### Creating Products
1. Navigate to the "Products" section in your dashboard
2. Click "Add New Product"
3. Enter product name, description, and price
4. Add an image URL (optional)
5. Toggle "Featured" to highlight important products
6. Click "Save Product"

#### Custom Payment Links
1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Create Payment Link"
3. Enter item details and buyer information
4. Share the generated link with your customer

#### Public Storefront
Your public storefront is available at `surepay.link/@yourusername`. It displays:
- Your brand information
- Featured products (up to 3)
- Trust metrics based on completed transactions

### Transaction Management

#### Viewing Transactions
1. Go to the "Transactions" section in your dashboard
2. See all your transactions with their current status
3. Filter by status or search for specific transactions

#### Marking Delivery
1. Find the transaction in your list
2. Click on the transaction to view details
3. Click "Mark as Delivered"
4. This starts the 24-hour buyer confirmation window

#### Handling Disputes
1. If a buyer opens a dispute, you'll receive a notification
2. Go to the "Disputes" section in your dashboard
3. View the dispute details and evidence provided
4. Upload your own evidence if needed
5. Wait for admin resolution

### Payout Management

#### Viewing Payouts
1. Navigate to the "Payouts" section in your dashboard
2. See all payout records with their status
3. Filter by status (pending, completed, failed)

#### Payout Statuses
- **Queued**: Payout is scheduled
- **Processing**: Payout is being processed
- **Succeeded**: Payout completed successfully
- **Failed**: Payout failed (you can retry)

#### Retrying Failed Payouts
1. Find the failed payout in your list
2. Click "Retry" if available
3. The system will attempt to process the payout again

## For Buyers

### Making a Payment

1. **Receive Checkout Link**
   - A vendor will send you a SurePay checkout link
   - This can be via WhatsApp, Instagram DM, or email

2. **Open the Link**
   - Click on the checkout link
   - Review the item details and price

3. **Enter Your Information**
   - Provide your name and email address
   - Add your WhatsApp number (optional but recommended)

4. **Pay with Paystack**
   - Click "Pay Now" to be redirected to Paystack
   - Complete payment using your preferred method
   - You'll be redirected back to a receipt page

### After Payment

#### Receiving Your Receipt
- After payment, you'll see a receipt page
- You'll also receive an email with a re-access link
- Save this link for future reference

#### Confirming Delivery
1. Once you receive your item, check your email for the receipt
2. Click on the "Confirm Delivery" button
3. This releases funds to the vendor

#### Reporting Issues
1. If there's a problem with your order:
   - Click "Report Issue" on your receipt page
   - Select the reason for your dispute
   - Provide details and any evidence
2. The vendor will be notified of your dispute
3. An admin will review and resolve the dispute

## Trust System

### Settlement Gravity Badge
Vendors display a trust badge showing:
- Number of protected sales
- Total volume of released transactions
- Successful payout count
- Last completed settlement date
- Resolved dispute count

### How Trust is Built
- Each successful transaction increases vendor trust metrics
- Buyers can view vendor trust history before purchasing
- Trust metrics are system-derived and cannot be faked

## Security Features

### Buyer Protection
- Funds are held securely until delivery is confirmed
- Dispute resolution process if issues arise
- Refund protection if vendor fails to deliver

### Vendor Protection
- Clear transaction history and audit trail
- Protected from false dispute claims
- Automated payout system

## Best Practices

### For Vendors
1. **Accurate Product Descriptions**
   - Provide detailed, honest product information
   - Include clear photos
   - Specify sizes, colors, and other variants

2. **Prompt Communication**
   - Respond to buyer inquiries quickly
   - Provide tracking information when available
   - Keep buyers updated on order status

3. **Reliable Fulfillment**
   - Ship orders promptly
   - Use reliable delivery methods
   - Provide tracking information

### For Buyers
1. **Verify Vendor Trust**
   - Check the vendor's trust badge before purchasing
   - Review completed transaction history
   - Look for recent successful settlements

2. **Clear Communication**
   - Provide accurate contact information
   - Communicate any special requirements
   - Report issues promptly if they arise

3. **Responsible Confirmation**
   - Only confirm delivery after receiving your item
   - Report issues immediately if they occur
   - Provide honest feedback

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

#### Payment Problems
- **Issue**: Payment fails or doesn't process
- **Solution**: Check your internet connection and try again. If problems persist, contact support

#### Accessing Receipt
- **Issue**: Can't find your receipt email
- **Solution**: Check spam/junk folders. Use the re-access link if you saved it

#### Dispute Resolution
- **Issue**: Dispute taking too long
- **Solution**: Disputes are typically resolved within 48 hours. Contact support for urgent issues

### Support

If you need help:
1. Check this user guide for answers
2. Contact support via the "Help" section in your dashboard
3. Email support@surepay.link for urgent issues

## Privacy and Data

### Data Collection
SurePay collects only the information necessary to process payments and resolve disputes:
- Transaction details
- Payment information (processed by Paystack)
- Communication records
- Dispute evidence

### Data Protection
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel only
- Data is retained according to legal requirements
